Breakfast Fruit Bowl

A healthy fruit and yogurt breakfast bowl!

Simple and fast to make!

Ingredients:

  • Fruit of your choosing
  • Coconut Yogurt Cup
  • Shredded Coconut
  • Allergy friendly Chocolate Chips

Method:

  1. Chop and prepare fruit
  2. Add Coconut Yogurt
  3. Top with shredded coconut and chocholate chips
  4. ENJOY!
